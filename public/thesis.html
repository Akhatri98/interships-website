Why This Site Was Built

I don't think mass applying to jobs is ever the most effective way to get an internship or a job, so my intended use case for this isn't to aggregate a bunch of listings and apply to all of them blindly. Instead, I want to find niche opportunities that might get overlooked by other boards and let people discover them easily.

This site was not made with any malicious intent; my goal is to simply make it easier for people to find internship opportunities that might otherwise be overlooked, not to exploit or misuse the data in any way.
